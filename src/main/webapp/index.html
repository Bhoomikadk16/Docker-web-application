Tourist Places in Karnataka

Hampi

Hampi, a UNESCO World Heritage site, is a historical marvel with ruins of Vijayanagara Empire. Explore ancient temples, royal enclosures, and stunning landscapes.

Mysore Palace

Mysore Palace, also known as Amba Vilas Palace, is a breathtaking example of Indo-Saracenic architecture. Witness its grandeur and rich history.

Gokarna

Gokarna is a coastal paradise with pristine beaches and laid-back vibes. Enjoy serene landscapes and temple visits.

Jog Falls

Jog Falls, one of India's tallest waterfalls, is a spectacular sight during monsoon. Experience the power of nature.

Coorg

Coorg, also known as Kodagu, is a lush hill station famous for coffee plantations, misty hills, and spice gardens.

Badami Cave Temples

Badami Cave Temples feature rock-cut caves with intricate carvings from the 6th and 7th centuries. Explore ancient Indian rock-cut architecture.

Aihole

Aihole is considered the cradle of stone architecture in South India with numerous temples and historical sites.
